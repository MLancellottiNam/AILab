How it works

Three steps, one flow

Assemble, map, and send. Dispatch handles the batch so your team stops copy-pasting documents by hand.

Bring your base document

A Word or TXT file with {{variables}}. Dispatch detects your data fields and your signature anchors automatically.

Map your data source

Drop a CSV, XLSX or JSON. Columns auto-match to your variables — 130 rows · 6 columns ready in seconds.

Send the whole batch

One decisive action generates a branded PDF per row and dispatches it for signing — with a live progress log, row by row.

Built for operations

Technical, but human

Serious with data, fast and frictionless in the hand. Everything the machine suggests stays reviewable.

Persists nothing

Tokens, documents and data live in session memory and are discarded. Nothing to disk, DB or logs.

Signature anchors

Signature fields are placed by named anchors baked into the PDF — invisible in the document, precise on send.

Live progress

A real-time log with badges, timestamps and stats. 128 of 130 sent — and it tells you exactly which 2 were skipped.

Your brand on every page

Colour, typography and title applied to each generated PDF, so a 130-doc batch still looks like you.

Four send types

Advanced signature, simple signature, certified email and certified SMS — one adapter, every Namirial stack.

Precise microcopy

Exact numbers, clear states, calm about your data. We name the AI plainly and never over-promise.

✦ AI · optional accelerator

The machine suggests. You confirm.

AI can extract brand colours from a brandbook and propose where signature fields go. It's never the hero — the manual path always works on its own.

The indigo rule: indigo means "a machine did this — review it." The moment you confirm, the element returns to the standard palette.
